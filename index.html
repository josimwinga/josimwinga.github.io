Mood Aesthetics

Mood Aesthetics is a simple interactive audio visualizer centered around four human emotional states: anger, sadness, happiness and tranquility. It aims to enhance the user’s current mood by pairing graphic animations and sonic melodies to the mood chosen by the user. The music and graphic animations were carefully chosen and designed to elicit a particular reaction from the user. The Angry function, for example, augments to the user’s rage by making use of loud music, bright red colors, and edgy shapes flashing across the screen. This function also features an interactive component wherein the user clicks the mouse rapidly and the color of the screen consequently turns into a brighter shade of red.
