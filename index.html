Open Data Durban

The Organization

Open Data Durban (ODD) is a non-profit civic technology lab in Durban, South Africa that works to drive civic engagement in eThekwini. They advocate for open data and open government within the eThekwini Municipality and develop data-driven civic technology tools and solutions. ODD aims to create positive social change which helps transform Durban into a more inclusive, productive, and happy city that is better governed.

While studying in Durban during the Spring of 2017, I interned at Open Data Durban while researching the the utilization of tehnology to create positive social change. While at ODD, I created a widget that provided community members with government information about medicine prices, redesigned the ODD suite of widgets, and built a website for the Ask Durban campaign.

MOSAIC+

The organization

Mosaic+ is a student-run organization which advocates for diversity in computer science. We strive towards an academic environment receptive to underrepresented minority students and faculty at Brown University. We are a group of committed and creative students working together to make proactive steps towards the celebration of diversity in the world of technology.

I am a founding member of the organization and served as the Branding Team Co-Chair for the past 2 years. As the Branding Team Co-Chair, I designed and developed the organization's website and blog.

THE ARTEMIS PROJECT

The Program

The Artemis Project is a 5 week summer program for rising 9th grade girls in the Providence area run by the Brown University CS Department. Each year, undergraduate women in the department coordinate the program.

As one of the found undergraduate coordinators, I helped to redesign the structure of the program, intensifying the focus on Python programming and adding an entreprenueral aspect.
